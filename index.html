About

This is The Bibliocloud Book—the manual for Bibliocloud, a business management system for book publishers. Bibliocloud has three main functions: tools to manage publishing activities, tools to manage workflow and tools to manage data. There are also tools to configure the system to tailor it to your precise needs. Bibliocloud is designed to help you ride out the digital revolution in style -- to not just survive, but thrive.

And this is not marketing spin. The folk behind Bibliocloud use it every day to run our multi-award-winning independent publishing company, Snowbooks. We eat our own dog food, as the saying goes. We have lived with the difficulties of modern book publishing for over a decade, and we built Bibliocloud out of that experience. Nowadays, Bibliocloud automates the drudge so we can use our time for creativity, innovation and growth.
